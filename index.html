Bio

Welcome to my sub-site where I store my Input Recalls and Lab assignments for the LING 360 class at UMBC!

I am pursuing a Computer Science major (Game Development Track) and an Applied Linguistics minor at UMBC. I've been interested in video games and was dedicated to learning how they work since I was a kid. In middle school I started programming in basic drag-and-drop languages like Scratch by MIT and then learned more complex text-based languages. I am most fluent in developing in C# and Unity, but I also program in Python, C++, and Java. I developed an interest for human languages in high school. That interest turned into an intrigue for linguistics when I started coming up with language learning app ideas. I went through many failed Google searches to finally learn about the terminology of Phonemes and Graphemes (See my prior blog post here).

I like to spend my spare time hanging out with friends; working on personal projects; roller blading; solving rubiks cubes; and reading books.
